FAQ

Questions? Answers.

String Free is a Telegram bot that acts as your AI-powered build control system. It uses Google Gemini to turn unstructured messages into structured tasks, provides inline approval buttons for structural changes, and converts Sentry error alerts into actionable improvement tasks with AI-proposed fixes. Think of it as your project manager that lives inside Telegram.

When you send a message to String Free, it's processed by Google Gemini. The AI analyzes your text — even messy, multi-topic messages — and extracts individual tasks with titles, priorities, and project assignments. It understands context from your previous messages and project structure to make intelligent decisions. You then review and approve or reject each task.

Absolutely. Messages are processed in real-time by Gemini and are not stored beyond what's needed for task creation. We use Telegram's encrypted bot API, and your data is never used for AI model training. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. You can delete all your data at any time with a single command.

Yes — Sentry integration is optional and available on Pro and Team plans. The core features — AI task parsing, smart approvals, and multi-project management — work independently of Sentry. Many users start with just task parsing and add error learning later when they need it.

String Free supports commands like /tasks to list open tasks, /projects to switch projects, /status for a project overview, /done to mark tasks complete, and /settings to configure preferences. But most of the time, you just type naturally — no commands needed.

Send /settings in Telegram and tap "Manage Subscription." You can downgrade or cancel instantly — no hoops, no retention flows. Your data stays accessible on the Free plan, and you can export everything as JSON at any time.
